KNOW YOUR LIBRARY

The Central Library of Narula Institute of Technology is more than just a collection of books; it is the intellectual heartbeat of our campus. Spanning across a vast area of 1244.87 sq. meters, our library is fully automated and designed to foster a culture of research and deep learning.

We take pride in our massive repository of over 83,000 resources, including printed books, international journals, and an extensive digital library. Students can access a wealth of knowledge through our E-Journals, E-Books, and multimedia CD-ROMs, available at their fingertips.

Whether you are looking for a quiet corner to study, technical references for your projects, or the latest periodicals to stay updated, the NiT Library provides a world-class environment equipped with modern facilities to support your academic excellence and future-focused growth.
