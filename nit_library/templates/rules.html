RULES AND REGULATIONS

Home / Rules and Regulations
Library rules are reorganized in accordance with the Library Policy since 2012. We expect all members to exercise self-discipline and respect while using the library.
1
The Library is open to registered members only. Valid membership card is mandatory.
2
Library timing: 8:00 am - 8:00 pm (Mon to Fri) & 9:45 am - 1:45 pm (Sat).
3
No bags, umbrellas, or personal books are allowed inside the library premises.
4
Consumption of food and use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited.
5
Fine for late return: Rs. 2 per day for each book.
6
Silence must be observed. No discussions are permitted inside the library.

⚠️ Pro Tip:

Loss of membership card must be reported to the librarian immediately to avoid misuse.
